WELCOME TO THE CUPCAKE SHOP!

BobAncent's first and finest cupcake shop

Here at The Cupcake Shop we bake from scratch each and every morning using only the highest quality, freshest ingredients available! Our bakers prepare 12-15 specialty cupcakes and other creative confections like muffins, cookies, seasonal bars, brownies, hand pies, granola, biscotti, ice cream sandwiches, and homemade marshmallows,(including some gluten free options!) for you to enjoy in the shop or for carry out.

Our menu features a variety of cupcakes daily, each topped with swirls of velvety icing in many traditional and modern flavors. The shop features new flavors each week and also offers sweet delicacies and custom cakes for any occasion or special event
